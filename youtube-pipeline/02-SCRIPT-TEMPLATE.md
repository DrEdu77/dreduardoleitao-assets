# Template de Script — 45 Minutos (Formato Listicle)

## Estrutura do vídeo (45 min total)

```
00:00 – 00:30  HOOK (30 seg) — A frase que prende
00:30 – 02:00  INTRO (90 seg) — Por que assistir até o fim
02:00 – 06:00  CAPÍTULO 1 — Fatos 1 a 6
06:00 – 12:00  CAPÍTULO 2 — Fatos 7 a 13  ← MID-ROLL AD
12:00 – 18:00  CAPÍTULO 3 — Fatos 14 a 20 ← MID-ROLL AD
18:00 – 24:00  CAPÍTULO 4 — Fatos 21 a 27 ← MID-ROLL AD
24:00 – 30:00  CAPÍTULO 5 — Fatos 28 a 34 ← MID-ROLL AD
30:00 – 36:00  CAPÍTULO 6 — Fatos 35 a 41 ← MID-ROLL AD
36:00 – 42:00  CAPÍTULO 7 — Fatos 42 a 47 ← MID-ROLL AD
42:00 – 44:00  CAPÍTULO 8 — Fatos 48 a 50 ← MID-ROLL AD
44:00 – 45:00  CTA (60 seg) — Subscribe + comment + próximo vídeo
```

---

## PROMPT PARA CLAUDE GERAR O SCRIPT

Cole este prompt direto no Claude para gerar qualquer script de 45 min:

```
You are a professional YouTube scriptwriter specializing in health and body education content.

Write a complete, engaging 45-minute YouTube script titled:
"[TÍTULO DO VÍDEO]"

FORMAT RULES:
- Total word count: 7,000–8,000 words (45 min at ~160 words/min narration)
- Hook (first 30 seconds): Start with a shocking statement or question. No "Hi everyone". No intro fluff.
- Introduction (90 seconds): Tease 3 surprising facts from the list. Tell viewers exactly why they must watch to the end.
- 50 facts total, organized into 8 chapters of 5–7 facts each
- Each fact: 3–4 sentences. First sentence = the fact. Second = why it's surprising. Third = real-world implication.
- Chapter titles must be intriguing (not just "Part 1")
- Add a [CHAPTER BREAK] marker at the start of each chapter
- Add a [MID-ROLL] marker at minute 6, 12, 18, 24, 30, 36, 42
- Each [MID-ROLL] transition line: "And speaking of [last topic], fact number X is even more surprising..."
- End with a CTA: Ask viewers to comment their most shocking fact, subscribe, and mention the next video title

TONE: Calm, confident, authoritative but accessible. Like a brilliant doctor friend explaining things simply.
No sensationalism. No clickbait language inside the video. The facts speak for themselves.

TOPIC: [DESCRIÇÃO DO TÓPICO EM INGLÊS]
```

## EXEMPLO — Script pronto: "50 Spine Facts"

### HOOK (00:00–00:30)
"Your spine makes decisions before your brain does. Right now, without you realizing it, 
your vertebral column is processing signals, controlling organs, and literally shaping 
your personality — and most people will live their entire lives without knowing any of this. 
Here are 50 facts about your spine that will fundamentally change how you see your own body."

### INTRO (00:30–02:00)
"We're going to cover everything — from why your spine shrinks during the day (fact number 7), 
to the hidden connection between your spine and your immune system that doctors rarely discuss 
(fact 23), all the way to the disturbing truth about what sitting does to your spinal discs 
after just 20 minutes (fact 41). Stay until the end — fact 50 is the one that genuinely 
surprises even medical professionals."

[Continua com os 50 fatos organizados em 8 capítulos]

## Dicas de Retenção

1. **Padrão de loop aberto**: Sempre termine um capítulo prometendo algo do próximo
   - "That was surprising — but what we found in chapter 4 is even harder to believe..."
2. **Números concretos**: "34 times stronger", "7 million nerve endings", "only 2% of people"
3. **Frases de ancoragem a cada 2 min**: "Pay attention to this one..." / "This is important..."
4. **Thumbnail keyword no script**: Dizer o título do vídeo nos primeiros 60 segundos (YouTube indexação)
