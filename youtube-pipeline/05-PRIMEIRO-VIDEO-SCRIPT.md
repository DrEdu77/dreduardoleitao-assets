# Script #1 — "50 Shocking Facts About Your Spine"

**Canal:** BodyTruth  
**Duração alvo:** 45 minutos  
**Palavras:** ~7.500  

---

## PROMPT COMPLETO PARA GERAR ESTE SCRIPT

Cole exatamente isso no Claude:

---

```
You are a professional YouTube scriptwriter specializing in health and body education.

Write a complete, engaging 45-minute YouTube script with the following specifications:

TITLE: "50 Shocking Facts About Your Spine That Will Change How You See Your Own Body"

FORMAT REQUIREMENTS:
- Total: 7,500–8,000 words
- 50 facts organized into 8 chapters
- Each fact: exactly 3–4 sentences (fact → why surprising → real implication)
- Add [CHAPTER X: CHAPTER TITLE] markers
- Add [MID-ROLL] markers at facts #7, #14, #21, #28, #35, #42, #48
- Each mid-roll transition: "And if that surprised you, wait until you hear fact number [next]..."

CHAPTER STRUCTURE:
Chapter 1 (Facts 1–6): "The Spine Is Not What You Think"
Chapter 2 (Facts 7–13): "Your Spine Has a Mind of Its Own"
Chapter 3 (Facts 14–20): "The Hidden Power of Your Vertebrae"
Chapter 4 (Facts 21–27): "What Your Spine Is Doing Right Now"
Chapter 5 (Facts 28–34): "Diseases, Disorders and Dark Truths"
Chapter 6 (Facts 35–41): "What Modern Life Is Doing To Your Spine"
Chapter 7 (Facts 42–47): "The Spine-Body Connection Nobody Talks About"
Chapter 8 (Facts 48–50): "The Three Facts That Will Change Everything"

HOOK (first 30 seconds — no greetings, start immediately):
Begin with: "Your spine makes decisions before your brain does..."

INTRO (30 sec – 2 min):
Tease facts #7, #23, and #41. Promise to reveal something most doctors don't mention.

CTA (last 60 seconds):
Ask viewers: "Which fact surprised you the most? Comment below."
Mention next video: "Next week, we're covering 50 facts about your heart that are even more alarming."
Ask to subscribe.

TONE: Calm, authoritative, like a brilliant doctor friend explaining complex things simply.
Use active voice. Short sentences. No jargon without explanation.
Source each fact category as "research shows", "studies suggest", "medical literature indicates".

Write the complete script now, all 7,500 words.
```

---

## Após gerar o script:

1. **Revisar** (5 min): checar se algum fato está errado ou exagerado
2. **Personalizar** 2-3 fatos com sua experiência real como osteopata (sem revelar identidade)
3. **Salvar** como `script-v01-spine-facts.txt`
4. **Gerar voz** no ElevenLabs com voz "Adam" ou "Josh"
5. **Editar vídeo** no CapCut seguindo o template
